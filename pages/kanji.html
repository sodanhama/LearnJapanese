Kanji

Most kanji characters have both onyomi and kunyomi readings.

Here are some basic kanji characters, not commonly used ones, as there are way too many of them.

Keep in mind, however, that in modern day Japan, kana have vastly replaced kanji usage due to the simplicity and ease of use with cellular devices.
